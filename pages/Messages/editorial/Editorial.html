MESSAGE FROM THE EDITORIAL

Dear readers,

It is with great delight that we present to you our first digital edition of 'Glimpses'. This has not only enabled us to cover the activities of the academic year 2017-2018 in great detail, but has also generally widened the scope of our presentation.

'Glimpses' will bring alive the memorable moments we captured on our lens, and highlight the varied activities of the school aimed at achieving all-round education of the students.

Thank you readers for your constant encouragement and support in our endeavour.

A very special 'thank you' to all the teachers and students who have worked tirelessly in putting 'Glimpses' together.

The Editorial Board
