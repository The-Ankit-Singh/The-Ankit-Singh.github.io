FROM THE PRINCIPAL'S DESK

“WHEN YOU SHIFT YOUR FOCUS FROM COMPETITION TO CONTRIBUTION LIFE BECOMES A CELEBRATION. NEVER TRY TO DEFEAT PEOPLE , JUST WIN THEIR HEARTS.” – GAUTAMA BUDDHA

As we prepare to bid farewell to 2017, we look into the future with a renewed sense of optimism. It has certainly been a memorable year for women, kicking off with almost five million strong women's March in January and culminating in a watershed moment that saw women from all around the world unite in defence of their dignity and right to dress however they pleased.

We also look back to trying to make our 'marians' realise their equality with the 'girl child' to transform, inspire and provide that all important feel-good factor, something we could all use a little more of right now. Glance through this digital magazine and all the events organised specially to give our Marians that sense of 'belonging' and developing their skills & talents along with academics to enhance their dignity and ability in all spheres of life.

Challenge yourself Marians in the true spirit of blessed Edmund rice to be better than what you are. Dream and realise those dreams into reality as you go forward steadily. Your parents and teachers are the main pillars of the school. Do remember that the quality of life cannot be raised unless we raise the texture of our thoughts and the depth of our understanding. The school is where you make your friends for life so do not forget that "a word of encouragement after failure is worth more than an hour of praise after success."

Each one in the magazine's 'editorial board' needs to be congratulated for their brilliant effort. A word of praise is due to all who have contributed to this magazine's success.

Here's to more magical history- making moments in the new academic year. Think positive and be positive and happy. God bless you Marians.

“HATRED DOES NOT CEASE BY HATRED , BUT ONLY BY LOVE;THIS IS THE ETERNAL RULE.” – GAUTAMA BUDDHA
