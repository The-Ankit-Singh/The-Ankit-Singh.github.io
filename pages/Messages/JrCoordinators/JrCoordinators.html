JUNIOR SCHOOL COORDINATOR'S MESSAGE

Dear Little Marians,

As we stand at the threshold of yet another new academic year, and look forward with gleaming eyes to new beginnings; I would like to extend my heartfelt gratitude to each one of you for making the year gone by, a memorable one.

I, on behalf of all Junior School Teachers whole heartedly appreciate the hard work and enthusiasm you put in to make the year a successful one.

So, dear little Marians..............Spread Your Wings and Fly!!!!

You’ve been a part of us

every moment of this school year. We were there

to watch you, celebrate with you,

encourage you, hold you.

Now, we see the beautiful person

You’ve grown to be, and we slowly step

into the gentle chapter of letting go.

We hold you close to our hearts and

bless you with the encouragement you so deserve.

We quietly let you go with hearts

fiercely longing to hold you closer.

Remember the gifts of faith, family and friends.

They will always be your sure foundation.

You’ve been a precious part of us every

single moment. And now with joyful gratitude

we celebrate your life. We open up our hearts

and let you fly.

So keep smiling and keep shining as you make all of us proud!!

With best wishes and prayers

Mrs. S. Brahmachari

(Junior School Coordinator)
